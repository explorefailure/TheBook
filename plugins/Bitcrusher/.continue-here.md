**Current Status:** Stage 2 Complete - Audio Engine Working

## What Is This Plugin?

A classic bitcrusher effect with bit depth and sample rate reduction. Creates crunchy lo-fi digital degradation, perfect for adding grit to drums, destroying synths, or creating retro video game vibes.
